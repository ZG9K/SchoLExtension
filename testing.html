Dark Mode Theme Selector

Select your SchoL Theme Here! System defaults uses your system theme setting, while light and dark mode override that setting for your preference.

Please Note: Not all text on SchoL will be compatible with dark mode, due to overridden custom formatting added to news/blog posts.
